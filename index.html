About the Data
Data source: Google Trends, keyword "epilare definitiva", Romania, January 2020 – 2025.
Normalization: Google Trends scores are normalized on a scale of 0–100, where 100 = peak relative interest within the selected time range — not an absolute search volume figure. All comparisons are relative to the highest point in the dataset (June 2023 = 100).
Visualization note: All charts use a Y-axis scaled to 125 to provide visual headroom above the normalized maximum, making trend differences easier to read. This is a deliberate design choice that does not distort the underlying data.

Analysis
Key Business Insights
☀️

Strong Summer Seasonality

June (avg 85.3) and July (avg 79.0) consistently represent the peak demand window across all years. Consumer interest is driven by beach season and warm-weather clothing. Businesses should maximize capacity and launch marketing campaigns in March–May to capture pre-peak intent before the rush.

📈

Sustained Market Growth

The Romanian market showed continuous growth from 2020 to 2023, with average annual interest rising from 56.3 to 77.3 — a gain of over 37%. A notable trend: 2024 Q1 scores significantly exceed all prior years (Jan: 87, Feb: 83, Mar: 81, Apr: 82), suggesting growing off-season consumer awareness.

❄️

Predictable Low Season

December consistently scores the lowest of any month (avg 52.5), followed by November (59.3), October (59.8), and September (60.2). This Q4 band is reliable and actionable — the ideal window for staff training, equipment maintenance, and building campaigns targeting the January rebound.

🔄

January Rebound Effect

January scores 74.2 on average — well above the December low of 52.5. This "new year" intent spike creates a secondary demand peak immediately after the weakest month. The effect has strengthened year-over-year: January 2024 reached a score of 87, the highest January on record.

Strategic Implications
Business Recommendations
  • 01

    Concentrate Marketing Spend in March–May

    Consumer intent builds significantly before peak. Campaigns launched in March–April capture high-intent customers before the June rush and allow bookings to fill in advance, when competition for attention is lower.

  • 02

    Maximize Booking Capacity for June–July

    These two months represent the highest-demand window of the year. Service slots should be fully booked in advance. Waitlists can convert to bookings in August, smoothing the post-peak drop-off.

  • 03

    Capitalize on the Strengthening January Effect

    January demand has grown year-over-year, reaching 87 in 2024. A campaign launched December 15–20 — positioning treatments as a New Year resolution — captures early-intent customers before January demand peaks and competitors activate.

  • 04

    Use Q4 for Retention and Loyalty Programs

    September–December is the reliable low season (avg 52.5–60.2). Rather than competing on acquisition, this window is ideal for loyalty incentives, package deals for existing clients, and referral programs that activate during peak season.

  • 05

    Investigate the 2025 H2 Decline

    2025 H2 scores (Jul: 66, Aug: 69, Sep: 57, Oct: 54, Nov: 61, Dec: 45) are notably lower than any prior year in the same months. This warrants investigation — possible causes include increased competition, changing search behavior, or market saturation in certain segments.
